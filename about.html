About Us

Bridging Turkish Manufacturing with the World

We're building the infrastructure that makes international B2B trade simple, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

SultanaExpress was founded with a simple but ambitious goal: to eliminate the friction in international B2B sourcing from Turkey. We believe that every manufacturer — regardless of size — deserves access to global markets, and every buyer deserves transparent, reliable sourcing options.

Turkey is one of the world's great manufacturing powerhouses, with over 10,000 textile factories alone, a Customs Union agreement with the EU, and a strategic geographic position bridging Europe and Asia. Yet connecting with Turkish suppliers has traditionally required trade show visits, cold outreach, and navigating language barriers with no guarantee of quality or reliability.

We're changing that.

What We Do

SultanaExpress is a B2B marketplace purpose-built for international trade with Turkish manufacturers. Our platform handles the entire procurement cycle:

  • Supplier Discovery — Browse verified manufacturers with detailed profiles, certifications, and performance ratings
  • Smart RFQ System — Create detailed Requests for Quote with industry-specific fields and receive competitive quotes
  • Quote Comparison — Compare offers from multiple suppliers with automatic scoring across price, quality, and delivery
  • Real-Time Communication — Built-in messaging linked to every RFQ and order for full context
  • Order Management — Track orders from confirmation through production, shipping, and delivery
  • Logistics Integration — Powered by Yusen Logistics with coverage across 47 countries

Our Values

Transparency

No hidden fees, no opaque pricing. Every cost, every commission, every process step is visible to both buyers and suppliers.

Trust

Every supplier undergoes verification. Performance tiers reward consistency. GDPR-compliant data handling protects everyone.

Accessibility

Free registration for all. Five-language support. Mobile apps for trade on the go. No barriers to getting started.

Quality

We prioritize suppliers with internationally recognized certifications — OEKO-TEX, GOTS, ISO 9001, GRS, and BSCI.

Our Focus Industries

While our platform supports multiple industries, we have deep expertise in:

  • Textiles & Fashion — Apparel, fabrics, accessories, home textiles
  • Machinery & Equipment — Industrial machinery, manufacturing equipment
  • Electronics — Consumer electronics and components
  • Agriculture — Turkish agricultural products and food processing
